
## Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing marketing campaign performance for a retail food & beverage company in collaboration with MarketMindz. The objective was to build an interactive BI dashboard that provides actionable insights into customer behavior, product performance, and campaign effectiveness.

The solution enables stakeholders to make data-driven marketing decisions by identifying high-performing campaigns, understanding customer segments, and uncovering key purchase drivers.


## Business Problem
The client is a small retail company that is still exploring:

Their target customers
Product demand patterns
Campaign effectiveness

They required a Business Intelligence solution to answer:

* How are recent marketing campaigns performing?
* How are different products performing?
* Who are the customers?
* What drives customer purchasing decisions?

## Key Insights
Campaign Performance
Campaign 6 generated the highest engagement:
* 334 purchases (highest)
* $0.33M revenue (top performing)

**Campaign 2** underperformed significantly across all metrics
Wine is the top-selling product (~$0.68M), followed by meat

**Insight**: Focus marketing budget on high-performing campaign patterns similar to Campaign 6.
## Product Performance
Revenue contribution is heavily skewed toward:
* **Wine**
* **Meat**

Low-performing categories:
Fruit, Sweets, Fish

**Insight**: Opportunity to optimize or reposition low-performing product categories.
## Customer Insights
* Total customers: 2240
* Average income: ~$52K
* Average age: 57 years

**Education**:

* Majority are Graduates (1127)

**Marital Status**:

* Mostly Married customers (864)

**Insight**: Core customer base = middle-aged, educated, married individuals
## Behavioral Insights
Older customers:
* Spend more on Wine
* Spend less on Meat

**Insight**: Customer preferences shift significantly with age → useful for targeted marketing.
## Key Drivers
Customers are 7.8x more likely to accept campaigns when:
* Income > $60K
Sales increase significantly when:
* Income range = ~$66K – $113K
Higher conversion when:
* Fewer kids/teens at home
* Lower recent web activity

**Insight**: Income is the strongest predictor of campaign success and sales growth
